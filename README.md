# 🚀 NEXUS AI
### Personal AI Operating System

> A JARVIS-style Personal AI Operating System that can see, hear, remember, understand, and act.

![NEXUS AI Banner](assets/banner.png)

---

## 🌟 Overview

NEXUS AI is an advanced Personal AI Operating System designed to go beyond traditional chatbots.

Instead of simply answering questions, NEXUS understands context, remembers important information, analyzes screens and documents, performs intelligent actions, and automates workflows.

Built with a local-first philosophy, NEXUS combines Voice AI, Vision Intelligence, Memory Systems, Agentic Automation, and Desktop Control into a single unified platform.

---

## 🎯 Vision

The goal of NEXUS AI is to create a true AI companion that:

- 🗣️ Communicates naturally through voice and chat
- 🧠 Remembers conversations and user preferences
- 👁️ Understands screens, images, and documents
- ⚡ Executes tasks autonomously
- 🤖 Plans and completes multi-step workflows
- 🔒 Respects privacy through local-first architecture

Think of it as your personal JARVIS for productivity, learning, development, and daily life.
