// what is nexus?

your always-on AI companion. 🛸

NEXUS AI is a production-grade, JARVIS-style personal AI operating system that lives on your desktop. It's not another chat window — it's an intelligent companion that communicates naturally, understands context, remembers everything important, analyzes your screen and files, executes real actions, and automates entire workflows. Always available. Always aware. Always yours.

🗣️ it talks

Natural, real-time voice conversations with a friendly, confident voice. Interrupt it mid-sentence — it handles it like a human would, online or offline.

🧠 it remembers

Long-term semantic memory stores your preferences, past conversations, and decisions — so context carries across days, weeks, and projects.

👁️ it sees

Understands your screen, screenshots, images, UI layouts, and documents using vision models and OCR. Ask "what am I looking at?" — it knows.

⚡ it acts

Opens apps, controls your computer, automates repetitive tasks, and chains workflows — with safety checks on every execution.

🤖 it thinks ahead

A planner-executor agent breaks complex goals into subtasks, executes them sequentially, tracks progress, and recovers from failures on its own.

🔒 it respects you

Local-first whenever possible and security-focused by design. Your data, your machine, your control.
